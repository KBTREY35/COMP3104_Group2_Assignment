George Brown College (GBC) is a public college located in Toronto, Ontario, Canada, established in 1967. 
It offers over 160 full-time programs, including diplomas, degrees, and certificates across various fields such as arts, business, health sciences, and technology. 
GBC has three main campuses—St. James, Casa Loma, and Skillsonyx—each equipped with modern facilities and labs to enhance student learning. 
The college emphasizes industry partnerships, providing students with co-op opportunities and hands-on experience, while also promoting community engagement and social responsibility. 
With a diverse student population, including many international students, GBC is situated in a vibrant urban environment that offers numerous cultural and employment opportunities.
